Influancer Widgets — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 19, 2026

1. Introduction

Influancer Widgets ("the App") is a mobile application that allows content creators to view their social media statistics through an iOS app and home screen widgets. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

2. Information We Collect

When you connect a social media platform (YouTube, Instagram, X/Twitter, or TikTok), the App accesses the following through official platform APIs:

  • Your public profile information (username, profile name)
  • Your public account statistics (follower/subscriber counts, view counts, likes, comments, impressions)

We access TikTok data through the TikTok API, including your public profile information (display name, avatar) and video performance metrics (views, likes, comments, shares). This data is used solely to display your statistics within the App.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to:

  • Display your social media statistics within the App and home screen widgets
  • Track changes in your metrics over time

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose other than displaying your own statistics back to you.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • Authentication tokens are stored securely using the iOS Keychain
  • We do not transmit your data to any external servers
  • We do not operate a backend server — all API communication happens directly between your device and the official platform APIs (Google, Meta, X Corp, TikTok/ByteDance)

5. Third-Party Services

The App communicates directly with the following platform APIs:

  • YouTube Data API (Google) — governed by Google's Privacy Policy
  • Instagram Graph API (Meta) — governed by Meta's Privacy Policy
  • X API (X Corp) — governed by X's Privacy Policy
  • TikTok API (ByteDance) — governed by TikTok's Privacy Policy

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, rent, or trade your personal information with any third parties. Your data stays on your device.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Your data remains on your device only
  • You can disconnect any platform at any time from the App's Settings screen, which deletes all stored tokens and data for that platform
  • Uninstalling the App removes all stored data from your device

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be indicated by the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
